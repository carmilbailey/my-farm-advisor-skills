# 2026 USA Sorghum Production Best Practices Report

> **Nationwide Guide for Sorghum Growers**: Grain/forage focus (plant May–Jun). USDA Jan 2026: 2025 prod **437M bu** ↑27% (72.6 bu/acre on 6.02M acres); 2026/27 est **~400–450M bu** (6.5M acres ↓, exports ~200M bu). Drought-tolerant row crop #5. Costs ~$500–700/acre (low inputs). **Skill**: FIPS → hybrid class, GDD, anthracnose risk, yield est. [esmis.nal.usda](https://esmis.nal.usda.gov/sites/default/release-files/795725/cropan26.pdf)

**Repo**: `docs/sorghum/2026_BEST_PRACTICES.md`. Field overlay: Dryland/irr. → pop/N recs.

## 1. 2026 Season Outlook [terrainag](https://www.terrainag.com/insights/planting-into-headwinds-early-2026-27-grain-outlook/)

- **Acreage**: ~6.5M planted (KS/TX/CO/OK/SD); grain 80%, forage/silage 20%. [sciencedirect](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0378429012004029)
- **Production**: 400–450M bu @ 65–75 bu/acre; resilient drought.
- **Exports**: ~200M bu (China variable); ethanol/feed domestic.
- **Prices**: $4–5/bu (CBOT); hedge May/Jul '26.
- **Policy**: Trump → biofuel/exports; sorghum ethanol ↑.
- **Risks**: China ↓; anthracnose humid. [frontiersin](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/plant-science/articles/10.3389/fpls.2021.641969/full)

**Breakeven**: $4.20/bu Plains dryland.

## 3. Phenology & GDU (Base 50°F) [nutrien-ekonomics](https://nutrien-ekonomics.com/news/sorghum-development-and-growth-staging/)

**9 Stages** (Veg: leaf #; Rep: % progressed):

| Stage                  | GDU (post-emerge) | Timing       | Events               |
| ---------------------- | ----------------- | ------------ | -------------------- |
| **VE**                 | 0                 | -            | Emergence.           |
| **V3–V6**              | 300–600           | 15–30 days   | 3–6 leaves; tillers. |
| **V8–VT**              | 800–1,200         | 40–50 days   | Boot; flag leaf.     |
| **R1 (50%)**           | 1,400–1,600       | 60–70 days   | Half plants headed.  |
| **R3 (Soft Dough)**    | 1,800–2,000       | 80–90 days   | Grain fill peak.     |
| **R6 (Physio Mature)** | **2,200–2,800**   | 100–120 days | Black layer.         |

**Yield Eq (On-Farm)**: [eupdate.agronomy.ksu](https://eupdate.agronomy.ksu.edu/article/grain-sorghum-yield-potential-an-on-farm-calculation-512-3)
\[ \text{Yield (bu/acre)} = \frac{\text{Heads/acre} \times 1,000 \div \text{Seed wt (mg/head)} }{56} \]

- Ex: 48k plants × 1.3 tillers × 1,500 seeds/head × 25.6 mg = 177 bu. [eupdate.agronomy.ksu](https://eupdate.agronomy.ksu.edu/article/grain-sorghum-yield-potential-an-on-farm-calculation-512-3)

## 4. Disease Forecast & IPM 2026 [frontiersin](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/plant-science/articles/10.3389/fpls.2021.641969/full)

**Threats**:

| Disease              | Regions            | Timing | Losses                                                                                                                | Controls                                                                                                                                        |
| -------------------- | ------------------ | ------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Anthracnose**      | Humid South/Plains | V6–R3  | 20–67% susc. [frontiersin](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/plant-science/articles/10.3389/fpls.2021.641969/full) | **Conv**: Azoxystrobin; resistant hybrids. **Org**: Rotation 2-yr, seed treat sulfur [niphm.gov](https://niphm.gov.in/IPMPackages/Sorghum.pdf). |
| **Grain Mold**       | Hot/humid harvest  | R5–R6  | Quality                                                                                                               | Dry <13%; timely harvest.                                                                                                                       |
| **Zonate Leaf Spot** | All                | Veg    | Defoliation                                                                                                           | Residue rot.                                                                                                                                    |

**IPM**: Scout V6 thresh 5%; resistant core. **2026**: Anthracnose ↑ wet spring. [frontiersin](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/plant-science/articles/10.3389/fpls.2021.641969/full)

**Insects**: Midge/headworm; thres 1/head R1. [niphm.gov](https://niphm.gov.in/IPMPackages/Sorghum.pdf)

## 6. Cropping Systems [cropscience.bayer](https://www.cropscience.bayer.us/articles/bayer/irrigation-scheduling-grain-sorghum)

**Forage**: Dual-purpose; cut boot.

**Pop**: 4–6 plants/ft row (60–90k/acre).

## 7. Stage-Specific Management

| Stage     | Water ("/day) | Actions                  | Risks                 |
