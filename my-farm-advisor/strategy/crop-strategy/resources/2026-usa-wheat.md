## 3. Phenology: Feekes & Zadoks Scales

**GDD** (Base 32–34°F winter; 40°F spring): Regional calendars normalize (↓CV 20%). [ui.adsabs.harvard](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2023AGUFMGC53H0903M/abstract)

**Key Stages** (Feekes/Zadoks):

| Feekes    | Zadoks | Timing (Winter) | Key Events                 |
| --------- | ------ | --------------- | -------------------------- |
| 2–3       | 12–21  | Oct–Nov         | Tillering (20–30 shoots).  |
| 6–7       | 30–39  | Mar–Apr         | Jointing; flag leaf.       |
| **10.5**  | **59** | May             | Heading; FHB window.       |
| 11.1–11.2 | 71–85  | Jun             | Milky → dough; rust scout. |
| 11.4      | 92     | Jul             | Ripening (physio mature).  |

**Yield Components**: [ahdb.org](https://ahdb.org.uk/knowledge-library/the-main-components-of-yield-in-wheat)
\[ \text{Yield (t/ha)} = \text{Ears/m}^2 \times \text{Grains/ear} \times \text{TGW (mg)} / 1,000 \]

- Ears: Tillering–GS39.
- Grains: GS39–61.
- TGW: Post-anthesis (bench: 460 ears, 48 gr/ear, 50mg). [ahdb.org](https://ahdb.org.uk/knowledge-library/the-main-components-of-yield-in-wheat)

**Planting**: Winter Sep 15–Oct 15 South → Nov North; spring Mar–Apr. [alseed](https://alseed.com/when-is-it-too-late-to-plant-oats-wheat-or-barley/)

## 6. Cropping Systems

**Winter Wheat (70% acres)**: Dryland Plains; rot sorghum/corn.
**Spring Wheat**: HRS North; higher N/protein. [dtnpf](https://www.dtnpf.com/agriculture/web/ag/news/article/2026/03/01/wheat-acreage-angst)
**Dryland (85%)**: Summer fallow; 18–20% moisture profile.
**Irrigated (PNW/Plains)**: 20–24"/season; K_c 0.3 GS20 → 1.15 GS60.
**Organic**: ↓ pop 20%; compost; rust-resistant. [sciencedirect](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2666188825004319)

**Pop**: 1.5–2M/acre winter; 1.8–2.2M spring.
